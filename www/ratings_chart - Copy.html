Make sense of your ratings using this chart. Based on observation of average ratings on over 2000 recordings including recordings from playback singers (anonymously added).
1-5 Below average in general population. But good news is can see very quick improvements with 30-40 mins of daily riyaz.
6-7 Just above average. Top 50% in general population but will not be recognized as a singer among friends and family. Basic daily riyaz of Sur & Taal for 1 year is enough to reach next level.
8-9 This is the level where people recognize talent in children. Top 20%. Most hobbyist singers start at this level right from birth. Can copy 60-80% of the tune. Do mostly sur taal riyaz.
10-12 Will be recognized as a singer among friends & family. Top 5%. Can copy 90% of the tune without much harkatein and does lots of mistakes in singing. Can take part in competitions at school, college level. Work on cleaning up the voice along with improving sur and taal.
13-15 Can sing professionally at local level - clubs/bars/bands/marriages. Work on cleaning up the voice & pronunciation, voice modulations & harkatein.
16-18 Without music anonymous recordings of playback singers are between 16 & 18 depending on song difficulty level. With karaoke & tech enhancements, you can sound pretty good.
19-20 Classical master. Years of classical training shows up in the form of extreme control at high pitch and high tempo.
